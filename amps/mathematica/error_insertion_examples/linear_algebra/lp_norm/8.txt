Given the following problem:

```

Find the norm of the vector $\, \left(-\frac{46}{5},3,-3,-\frac{28}{5},-\frac{29}{5},-\frac{18}{5},9\right)\,$ :

$\left\| \, \left(-\frac{46}{5},3,-3,-\frac{28}{5},-\frac{29}{5},-\frac{18}{5},9\right)\, \right\|$

```

And correct solution:

```

1. The formula for the 7-dimensional Euclidean norm comes from the 7-dimensional Pythagorean theorem:

$\left\| \, \left(v_1,v_2,v_3,v_4,v_5,v_6,v_7\right)\, \right\| =\sqrt{v_1^2+v_2^2+v_3^2+v_4^2+v_5^2+v_6^2+v_7^2}$

2. Substitute $\, \left(-\frac{46}{5},3,-3,-\frac{28}{5},-\frac{29}{5},-\frac{18}{5},9\right)\,$ into the formula:

Answer: $\left\| \, \left(-\frac{46}{5},3,-3,-\frac{28}{5},-\frac{29}{5},-\frac{18}{5},9\right)\, \right\| =\sqrt{\left(\frac{-46}{5}\right)^2+3^2+(-3)^2+\left(\frac{-28}{5}\right)^2+\left(\frac{-29}{5}\right)^2+\left(\frac{-18}{5}\right)^2+9^2}=2 \sqrt{\frac{327}{5}}$

```

Insert an error into step 1.
