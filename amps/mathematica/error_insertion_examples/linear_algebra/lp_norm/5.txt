Given the following problem:

```

Find the norm of the vector $\, \left(\frac{19}{10},-\frac{44}{5},-\frac{89}{10},-2,\frac{31}{5},\frac{49}{5},\frac{43}{5}\right)\,$ :

$\left\| \, \left(\frac{19}{10},-\frac{44}{5},-\frac{89}{10},-2,\frac{31}{5},\frac{49}{5},\frac{43}{5}\right)\, \right\|$

```

And correct solution:

```

1. The formula for the 7-dimensional Euclidean norm comes from the 7-dimensional Pythagorean theorem:

$\left\| \, \left(v_1,v_2,v_3,v_4,v_5,v_6,v_7\right)\, \right\| =\sqrt{v_1^2+v_2^2+v_3^2+v_4^2+v_5^2+v_6^2+v_7^2}$

2. Substitute $\, \left(\frac{19}{10},-\frac{44}{5},-\frac{89}{10},-2,\frac{31}{5},\frac{49}{5},\frac{43}{5}\right)\,$ into the formula:

Answer: $\left\| \, \left(\frac{19}{10},-\frac{44}{5},-\frac{89}{10},-2,\frac{31}{5},\frac{49}{5},\frac{43}{5}\right)\, \right\| =\sqrt{\left(\frac{19}{10}\right)^2+\left(\frac{-44}{5}\right)^2+\left(\frac{-89}{10}\right)^2+(-2)^2+\left(\frac{31}{5}\right)^2+\left(\frac{49}{5}\right)^2+\left(\frac{43}{5}\right)^2}=\sqrt{\frac{3727}{10}}$

```

Insert an error into step 2.
