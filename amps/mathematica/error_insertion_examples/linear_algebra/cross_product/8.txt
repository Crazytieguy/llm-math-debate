Given the following problem:

```

Compute the following cross product:

$\, \left(4,\frac{73}{10},\frac{3}{2}\right)\, \times \, \left(-\frac{47}{5},\frac{1}{10},\frac{32}{5}\right)\,$

```

And correct solution:

```

1. Construct a matrix where the first row contains unit vectors $\hat{\text{i}}, \hat{\text{j}},\text{ and }\hat{\text{k}};\text{ and the second and third rows are made of vectors }\, \left(4,\frac{73}{10},\frac{3}{2}\right)\,\text{  and }\, \left(-\frac{47}{5},\frac{1}{10},\frac{32}{5}\right)\,$ :

$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
\hat{\text{i}} & \hat{\text{j}} & \hat{\text{k}} \\
 4 & \frac{73}{10} & \frac{3}{2} \\
 -\frac{47}{5} & \frac{1}{10} & \frac{32}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right)$

2. Take the determinant of this matrix:

$\left| 
\begin{array}{ccc}
\hat{\text{i}} & \hat{\text{j}} & \hat{\text{k}} \\
 4 & \frac{73}{10} & \frac{3}{2} \\
 -\frac{47}{5} & \frac{1}{10} & \frac{32}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right|$

3. Expand with respect to row $1:$

= $\left| 
\begin{array}{ccc}
\hat{\text{i}} & \hat{\text{j}} & \hat{\text{k}} \\
 4 & \frac{73}{10} & \frac{3}{2} \\
 -\frac{47}{5} & \frac{1}{10} & \frac{32}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right|$

4. The determinant of the matrix $\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
\hat{\text{i}} & \hat{\text{j}} & \hat{\text{k}} \\
 4 & \frac{73}{10} & \frac{3}{2} \\
 -\frac{47}{5} & \frac{1}{10} & \frac{32}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right)\text{ is given by }\hat{\text{i}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
\frac{73}{10} & \frac{3}{2} \\
 \frac{1}{10} & \frac{32}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right| +\left(-\hat{\text{j}}\right) \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
4 & \frac{3}{2} \\
 -\frac{47}{5} & \frac{32}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right| +\hat{\text{k}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
4 & \frac{73}{10} \\
 -\frac{47}{5} & \frac{1}{10} \\
\end{array}
\right|$ :

= $\hat{\text{i}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
\frac{73}{10} & \frac{3}{2} \\
 \frac{1}{10} & \frac{32}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right| +\left(-\hat{\text{j}}\right) \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
4 & \frac{3}{2} \\
 -\frac{47}{5} & \frac{32}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right| +\hat{\text{k}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
4 & \frac{73}{10} \\
 -\frac{47}{5} & \frac{1}{10} \\
\end{array}
\right|$

5. $\hat{\text{i}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
\frac{73}{10} & \frac{3}{2} \\
 \frac{1}{10} & \frac{32}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right| =\hat{\text{i}} \left(\frac{73\ 32}{10\ 5}-\frac{3}{2\ 10}\right)=\frac{\hat{\text{i}} 4657}{100}=\frac{4657 \hat{\text{i}}}{100}$:

= $\frac{4657 \hat{\text{i}}}{100}+\left(-\hat{\text{j}}\right) \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
4 & \frac{3}{2} \\
 -\frac{47}{5} & \frac{32}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right| +\hat{\text{k}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
4 & \frac{73}{10} \\
 -\frac{47}{5} & \frac{1}{10} \\
\end{array}
\right|$

6. $-\hat{\text{j}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
4 & \frac{3}{2} \\
 -\frac{47}{5} & \frac{32}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right| =-\hat{\text{j}} \left(\frac{4\ 32}{5}-\frac{3 (-47)}{2\ 5}\right)=\frac{-\hat{\text{j}} 397}{10}=-\frac{397 \hat{\text{j}}}{10}$:

= $\frac{4657 \hat{\text{i}}}{100}+-\frac{397 \hat{\text{j}}}{10}+\hat{\text{k}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
4 & \frac{73}{10} \\
 -\frac{47}{5} & \frac{1}{10} \\
\end{array}
\right|$

7. $\hat{\text{k}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
4 & \frac{73}{10} \\
 -\frac{47}{5} & \frac{1}{10} \\
\end{array}
\right| =\hat{\text{k}} \left(\frac{4}{10}-\frac{73 (-47)}{10\ 5}\right)=\frac{\hat{\text{k}} 3451}{50}=\frac{3451 \hat{\text{k}}}{50}$:

= $\frac{4657 \hat{\text{i}}}{100}-\frac{397 \hat{\text{j}}}{10}+\frac{3451 \hat{\text{k}}}{50}$

8. Order the terms in a more natural way:

= $\frac{4657 \hat{\text{i}}}{100}-\frac{397 \hat{\text{j}}}{10}+\frac{3451 \hat{\text{k}}}{50}$

9. $\frac{4657 \hat{\text{i}}}{100}-\frac{397 \hat{\text{j}}}{10}+\frac{3451 \hat{\text{k}}}{50}=\, \left(\frac{4657}{100},-\frac{397}{10},\frac{3451}{50}\right)\,$ :

Answer: $\, \left(\frac{4657}{100},-\frac{397}{10},\frac{3451}{50}\right)\,$

```
