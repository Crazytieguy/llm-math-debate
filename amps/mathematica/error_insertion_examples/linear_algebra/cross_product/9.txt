Given the following problem:

```

Compute the following cross product:

$\, (-6,0,8)\, \times \, (0,3,7)\,$

```

And correct solution:

```

1. Construct a matrix where the first row contains unit vectors $\hat{\text{i}}, \hat{\text{j}},\text{ and }\hat{\text{k}};\text{ and the second and third rows are made of vectors }\, (-6,0,8)\,\text{  and }\, (0,3,7)\,$ :

$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
\hat{\text{i}} & \hat{\text{j}} & \hat{\text{k}} \\
 -6 & 0 & 8 \\
 0 & 3 & 7 \\
\end{array}
\right)$

2. Take the determinant of this matrix:

$\left| 
\begin{array}{ccc}
\hat{\text{i}} & \hat{\text{j}} & \hat{\text{k}} \\
 -6 & 0 & 8 \\
 0 & 3 & 7 \\
\end{array}
\right|$

3. Expand with respect to row $1:$

= $\left| 
\begin{array}{ccc}
\hat{\text{i}} & \hat{\text{j}} & \hat{\text{k}} \\
 -6 & 0 & 8 \\
 0 & 3 & 7 \\
\end{array}
\right|$

4. The determinant of the matrix $\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
\hat{\text{i}} & \hat{\text{j}} & \hat{\text{k}} \\
 -6 & 0 & 8 \\
 0 & 3 & 7 \\
\end{array}
\right)\text{ is given by }\hat{\text{i}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
0 & 8 \\
 3 & 7 \\
\end{array}
\right| +\left(-\hat{\text{j}}\right) \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-6 & 8 \\
 0 & 7 \\
\end{array}
\right| +\hat{\text{k}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-6 & 0 \\
 0 & 3 \\
\end{array}
\right|$ :

= $\hat{\text{i}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
0 & 8 \\
 3 & 7 \\
\end{array}
\right| +\left(-\hat{\text{j}}\right) \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-6 & 8 \\
 0 & 7 \\
\end{array}
\right| +\hat{\text{k}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-6 & 0 \\
 0 & 3 \\
\end{array}
\right|$

5. $\hat{\text{i}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
0 & 8 \\
 3 & 7 \\
\end{array}
\right| =\hat{\text{i}} (0\ 7-8\ 3)=\hat{\text{i}} (-24)=-24 \hat{\text{i}}$:

= $-24 \hat{\text{i}}+\left(-\hat{\text{j}}\right) \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-6 & 8 \\
 0 & 7 \\
\end{array}
\right| +\hat{\text{k}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-6 & 0 \\
 0 & 3 \\
\end{array}
\right|$

6. $-\hat{\text{j}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-6 & 8 \\
 0 & 7 \\
\end{array}
\right| =-\hat{\text{j}} ((-6)\, \times \, 7-8\ 0)=-\hat{\text{j}} (-42)=42 \hat{\text{j}}$:

= $-24 \hat{\text{i}}+42 \hat{\text{j}}+\hat{\text{k}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-6 & 0 \\
 0 & 3 \\
\end{array}
\right|$

7. $\hat{\text{k}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-6 & 0 \\
 0 & 3 \\
\end{array}
\right| =\hat{\text{k}} ((-6)\, \times \, 3-0\ 0)=\hat{\text{k}} (-18)=-18 \hat{\text{k}}$:

= $-24 \hat{\text{i}}+42 \hat{\text{j}}+-18 \hat{\text{k}}$

8. Order the terms in a more natural way:

= $-24 \hat{\text{i}}+42 \hat{\text{j}}-18 \hat{\text{k}}$

9. $-24 \hat{\text{i}}+42 \hat{\text{j}}-18 \hat{\text{k}}=\, (-24,42,-18)\,$ :

Answer: $\, (-24,42,-18)\,$

```

Insert an error into step 2.
