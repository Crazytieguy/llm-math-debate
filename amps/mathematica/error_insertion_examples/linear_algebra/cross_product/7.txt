Given the following problem:

```

Compute the following cross product:

$\, \left(-\frac{20}{3},-\frac{5}{3},\frac{4}{3}\right)\, \times \, \left(-7,3,-\frac{22}{3}\right)\,$

```

And correct solution:

```

1. Construct a matrix where the first row contains unit vectors $\hat{\text{i}}, \hat{\text{j}},\text{ and }\hat{\text{k}};\text{ and the second and third rows are made of vectors }\, \left(-\frac{20}{3},-\frac{5}{3},\frac{4}{3}\right)\,\text{  and }\, \left(-7,3,-\frac{22}{3}\right)\,$ :

$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
\hat{\text{i}} & \hat{\text{j}} & \hat{\text{k}} \\
 -\frac{20}{3} & -\frac{5}{3} & \frac{4}{3} \\
 -7 & 3 & -\frac{22}{3} \\
\end{array}
\right)$

2. Take the determinant of this matrix:

$\left| 
\begin{array}{ccc}
\hat{\text{i}} & \hat{\text{j}} & \hat{\text{k}} \\
 -\frac{20}{3} & -\frac{5}{3} & \frac{4}{3} \\
 -7 & 3 & -\frac{22}{3} \\
\end{array}
\right|$

3. Expand with respect to row $1:$

= $\left| 
\begin{array}{ccc}
\hat{\text{i}} & \hat{\text{j}} & \hat{\text{k}} \\
 -\frac{20}{3} & -\frac{5}{3} & \frac{4}{3} \\
 -7 & 3 & -\frac{22}{3} \\
\end{array}
\right|$

4. The determinant of the matrix $\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
\hat{\text{i}} & \hat{\text{j}} & \hat{\text{k}} \\
 -\frac{20}{3} & -\frac{5}{3} & \frac{4}{3} \\
 -7 & 3 & -\frac{22}{3} \\
\end{array}
\right)\text{ is given by }\hat{\text{i}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-\frac{5}{3} & \frac{4}{3} \\
 3 & -\frac{22}{3} \\
\end{array}
\right| +\left(-\hat{\text{j}}\right) \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-\frac{20}{3} & \frac{4}{3} \\
 -7 & -\frac{22}{3} \\
\end{array}
\right| +\hat{\text{k}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-\frac{20}{3} & -\frac{5}{3} \\
 -7 & 3 \\
\end{array}
\right|$ :

= $\hat{\text{i}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-\frac{5}{3} & \frac{4}{3} \\
 3 & -\frac{22}{3} \\
\end{array}
\right| +\left(-\hat{\text{j}}\right) \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-\frac{20}{3} & \frac{4}{3} \\
 -7 & -\frac{22}{3} \\
\end{array}
\right| +\hat{\text{k}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-\frac{20}{3} & -\frac{5}{3} \\
 -7 & 3 \\
\end{array}
\right|$

5. $\hat{\text{i}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-\frac{5}{3} & \frac{4}{3} \\
 3 & -\frac{22}{3} \\
\end{array}
\right| =\hat{\text{i}} \left(\left(-\frac{5}{3}\right)\, \left(-\frac{22}{3}\right)-\frac{4\ 3}{3}\right)=\frac{\hat{\text{i}} 74}{9}=\frac{74 \hat{\text{i}}}{9}$:

= $\frac{74 \hat{\text{i}}}{9}+\left(-\hat{\text{j}}\right) \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-\frac{20}{3} & \frac{4}{3} \\
 -7 & -\frac{22}{3} \\
\end{array}
\right| +\hat{\text{k}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-\frac{20}{3} & -\frac{5}{3} \\
 -7 & 3 \\
\end{array}
\right|$

6. $-\hat{\text{j}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-\frac{20}{3} & \frac{4}{3} \\
 -7 & -\frac{22}{3} \\
\end{array}
\right| =-\hat{\text{j}} \left(\left(-\frac{20}{3}\right)\, \left(-\frac{22}{3}\right)-\frac{4 (-7)}{3}\right)=\frac{-\hat{\text{j}} 524}{9}=-\frac{524 \hat{\text{j}}}{9}$:

= $\frac{74 \hat{\text{i}}}{9}+-\frac{524 \hat{\text{j}}}{9}+\hat{\text{k}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-\frac{20}{3} & -\frac{5}{3} \\
 -7 & 3 \\
\end{array}
\right|$

7. $\hat{\text{k}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-\frac{20}{3} & -\frac{5}{3} \\
 -7 & 3 \\
\end{array}
\right| =\hat{\text{k}} \left(\left(-\frac{20}{3}\right)\, \times \, 3-\left(-\frac{5}{3}\right)\, (-7)\right)=\frac{\hat{\text{k}} (-95)}{3}=-\frac{95 \hat{\text{k}}}{3}$:

= $\frac{74 \hat{\text{i}}}{9}-\frac{524 \hat{\text{j}}}{9}+-\frac{95 \hat{\text{k}}}{3}$

8. Order the terms in a more natural way:

= $\frac{74 \hat{\text{i}}}{9}-\frac{524 \hat{\text{j}}}{9}-\frac{95 \hat{\text{k}}}{3}$

9. $\frac{74 \hat{\text{i}}}{9}-\frac{524 \hat{\text{j}}}{9}-\frac{95 \hat{\text{k}}}{3}=\, \left(\frac{74}{9},-\frac{524}{9},-\frac{95}{3}\right)\,$ :

Answer: $\, \left(\frac{74}{9},-\frac{524}{9},-\frac{95}{3}\right)\,$

```
