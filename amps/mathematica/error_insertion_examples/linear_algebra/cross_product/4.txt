Given the following problem:

```

Compute the following cross product:

$\, \left(-\frac{36}{5},-\frac{48}{5},\frac{46}{5}\right)\, \times \, \left(-5,-3,\frac{26}{5}\right)\,$

```

And correct solution:

```

1. Construct a matrix where the first row contains unit vectors $\hat{\text{i}}, \hat{\text{j}},\text{ and }\hat{\text{k}};\text{ and the second and third rows are made of vectors }\, \left(-\frac{36}{5},-\frac{48}{5},\frac{46}{5}\right)\,\text{  and }\, \left(-5,-3,\frac{26}{5}\right)\,$ :

$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
\hat{\text{i}} & \hat{\text{j}} & \hat{\text{k}} \\
 -\frac{36}{5} & -\frac{48}{5} & \frac{46}{5} \\
 -5 & -3 & \frac{26}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right)$

2. Take the determinant of this matrix:

$\left| 
\begin{array}{ccc}
\hat{\text{i}} & \hat{\text{j}} & \hat{\text{k}} \\
 -\frac{36}{5} & -\frac{48}{5} & \frac{46}{5} \\
 -5 & -3 & \frac{26}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right|$

3. Expand with respect to row $1:$

= $\left| 
\begin{array}{ccc}
\hat{\text{i}} & \hat{\text{j}} & \hat{\text{k}} \\
 -\frac{36}{5} & -\frac{48}{5} & \frac{46}{5} \\
 -5 & -3 & \frac{26}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right|$

4. The determinant of the matrix $\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
\hat{\text{i}} & \hat{\text{j}} & \hat{\text{k}} \\
 -\frac{36}{5} & -\frac{48}{5} & \frac{46}{5} \\
 -5 & -3 & \frac{26}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right)\text{ is given by }\hat{\text{i}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-\frac{48}{5} & \frac{46}{5} \\
 -3 & \frac{26}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right| +\left(-\hat{\text{j}}\right) \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-\frac{36}{5} & \frac{46}{5} \\
 -5 & \frac{26}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right| +\hat{\text{k}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-\frac{36}{5} & -\frac{48}{5} \\
 -5 & -3 \\
\end{array}
\right|$ :

= $\hat{\text{i}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-\frac{48}{5} & \frac{46}{5} \\
 -3 & \frac{26}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right| +\left(-\hat{\text{j}}\right) \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-\frac{36}{5} & \frac{46}{5} \\
 -5 & \frac{26}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right| +\hat{\text{k}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-\frac{36}{5} & -\frac{48}{5} \\
 -5 & -3 \\
\end{array}
\right|$

5. $\hat{\text{i}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-\frac{48}{5} & \frac{46}{5} \\
 -3 & \frac{26}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right| =\hat{\text{i}} \left(\left(-\frac{48}{5}\right)\, \times \, \frac{26}{5}-\frac{46 (-3)}{5}\right)=\frac{\hat{\text{i}} (-558)}{25}=-\frac{558 \hat{\text{i}}}{25}$:

= $-\frac{558 \hat{\text{i}}}{25}+\left(-\hat{\text{j}}\right) \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-\frac{36}{5} & \frac{46}{5} \\
 -5 & \frac{26}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right| +\hat{\text{k}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-\frac{36}{5} & -\frac{48}{5} \\
 -5 & -3 \\
\end{array}
\right|$

6. $-\hat{\text{j}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-\frac{36}{5} & \frac{46}{5} \\
 -5 & \frac{26}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right| =-\hat{\text{j}} \left(\left(-\frac{36}{5}\right)\, \times \, \frac{26}{5}-\frac{46 (-5)}{5}\right)=\frac{-\hat{\text{j}} 214}{25}=-\frac{214 \hat{\text{j}}}{25}$:

= $\frac{-558 \hat{\text{i}}}{25}+-\frac{214 \hat{\text{j}}}{25}+\hat{\text{k}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-\frac{36}{5} & -\frac{48}{5} \\
 -5 & -3 \\
\end{array}
\right|$

7. $\hat{\text{k}} \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-\frac{36}{5} & -\frac{48}{5} \\
 -5 & -3 \\
\end{array}
\right| =\hat{\text{k}} \left(\left(-\frac{36}{5}\right)\, (-3)-\left(-\frac{48}{5}\right)\, (-5)\right)=\frac{\hat{\text{k}} (-132)}{5}=-\frac{132 \hat{\text{k}}}{5}$:

= $\frac{-558 \hat{\text{i}}}{25}-\frac{214 \hat{\text{j}}}{25}+-\frac{132 \hat{\text{k}}}{5}$

8. Order the terms in a more natural way:

= $-\frac{558 \hat{\text{i}}}{25}-\frac{214 \hat{\text{j}}}{25}-\frac{132 \hat{\text{k}}}{5}$

9. $-\frac{558 \hat{\text{i}}}{25}-\frac{214 \hat{\text{j}}}{25}-\frac{132 \hat{\text{k}}}{5}=\, \left(-\frac{558}{25},-\frac{214}{25},-\frac{132}{5}\right)\,$ :

Answer: $\, \left(-\frac{558}{25},-\frac{214}{25},-\frac{132}{5}\right)\,$

```
