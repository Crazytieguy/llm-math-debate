Given the following problem:

```

Find all the eigenvalues of the matrix $M:$

$M=\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
3 & -6 \\
 \frac{7}{2} & \frac{11}{2} \\
\end{array}
\right)$

```

And correct solution:

```

1. Find $\lambda \in \mathbb{C}\text{ such that }M v=\lambda  v\text{ for some nonzero vector }v:$

$M v=\lambda  v$

2. Rewrite the equation $M v=\lambda  v\text{ as }(M-\mathbb{I} \lambda ) v=0:$

$(M-\mathbb{I} \lambda ) v=0$

3. Find all $\lambda\text{  such that }| M-\mathbb{I} \lambda | =0:$

$| M-\mathbb{I} \lambda | =0$

4. $\begin{array}{lll}
M-\mathbb{I} \lambda  & = & \left(
\begin{array}{cc}
3 & -6 \\
 \frac{7}{2} & \frac{11}{2} \\
\end{array}
\right)-\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
1 & 0 \\
 0 & 1 \\
\end{array}
\right) \lambda  \\
\text{  }& = & \left(
\begin{array}{cc}
3-\lambda  & -6 \\
 \frac{7}{2} & \frac{11}{2}-\lambda  \\
\end{array}
\right) \\
\end{array}$

$\left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
3-\lambda  & -6 \\
 \frac{7}{2} & \frac{11}{2}-\lambda  \\
\end{array}
\right| =0$

5. $\left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
3-\lambda  & -6 \\
 \frac{7}{2} & \frac{11}{2}-\lambda  \\
\end{array}
\right| =\lambda ^2-\frac{17 \lambda }{2}+\frac{75}{2}$:

$\lambda ^2-\frac{17 \lambda }{2}+\frac{75}{2}=0$

6. Subtract $\frac{75}{2}$ from both sides:

$\lambda ^2-\frac{17 \lambda }{2}=-\frac{75}{2}$

7. Add $\frac{289}{16}$ to both sides:

$\lambda ^2-\frac{17 \lambda }{2}+\frac{289}{16}=-\frac{311}{16}$

8. Write the left hand side as a square:

$\left(\lambda -\frac{17}{4}\right)^2=-\frac{311}{16}$

9. Take the square root of both sides:

$\lambda -\frac{17}{4}=\frac{i \sqrt{311}}{4}\text{ or }\lambda -\frac{17}{4}=-\frac{i \sqrt{311}}{4}$

10. Add $\frac{17}{4}$ to both sides:

$\lambda =\frac{17}{4}+\frac{i \sqrt{311}}{4}\text{ or }\lambda -\frac{17}{4}=-\frac{i \sqrt{311}}{4}$

11. Add $\frac{17}{4}$ to both sides:

Answer: $\lambda =\frac{17}{4}+\frac{i \sqrt{311}}{4}\text{ or }\lambda =\frac{17}{4}-\frac{i \sqrt{311}}{4}$

```

Insert an error into step 2.
