Given the following problem:

```

Find all the eigenvalues of the matrix $M:$

$M=\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
-\frac{9}{2} & \frac{17}{4} \\
 -\frac{35}{4} & -\frac{5}{4} \\
\end{array}
\right)$

```

And correct solution:

```

1. Find $\lambda \in \mathbb{C}\text{ such that }M v=\lambda  v\text{ for some nonzero vector }v:$

$M v=\lambda  v$

2. Rewrite the equation $M v=\lambda  v\text{ as }(M-\mathbb{I} \lambda ) v=0:$

$(M-\mathbb{I} \lambda ) v=0$

3. Find all $\lambda\text{  such that }| M-\mathbb{I} \lambda | =0:$

$| M-\mathbb{I} \lambda | =0$

4. $\begin{array}{lll}
M-\mathbb{I} \lambda  & = & \left(
\begin{array}{cc}
-\frac{9}{2} & \frac{17}{4} \\
 -\frac{35}{4} & -\frac{5}{4} \\
\end{array}
\right)-\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
1 & 0 \\
 0 & 1 \\
\end{array}
\right) \lambda  \\
\text{  }& = & \left(
\begin{array}{cc}
-\lambda -\frac{9}{2} & \frac{17}{4} \\
 -\frac{35}{4} & -\lambda -\frac{5}{4} \\
\end{array}
\right) \\
\end{array}$

$\left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-\lambda -\frac{9}{2} & \frac{17}{4} \\
 -\frac{35}{4} & -\lambda -\frac{5}{4} \\
\end{array}
\right| =0$

5. $\left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-\lambda -\frac{9}{2} & \frac{17}{4} \\
 -\frac{35}{4} & -\lambda -\frac{5}{4} \\
\end{array}
\right| =\lambda ^2+\frac{23 \lambda }{4}+\frac{685}{16}$:

$\lambda ^2+\frac{23 \lambda }{4}+\frac{685}{16}=0$

6. Subtract $\frac{685}{16}$ from both sides:

$\lambda ^2+\frac{23 \lambda }{4}=-\frac{685}{16}$

7. Add $\frac{529}{64}$ to both sides:

$\lambda ^2+\frac{23 \lambda }{4}+\frac{529}{64}=-\frac{2211}{64}$

8. Write the left hand side as a square:

$\left(\lambda +\frac{23}{8}\right)^2=-\frac{2211}{64}$

9. Take the square root of both sides:

$\lambda +\frac{23}{8}=\frac{i \sqrt{2211}}{8}\text{ or }\lambda +\frac{23}{8}=-\frac{i \sqrt{2211}}{8}$

10. Subtract $\frac{23}{8}$ from both sides:

$\lambda =\frac{i \sqrt{2211}}{8}-\frac{23}{8}\text{ or }\lambda +\frac{23}{8}=-\frac{i \sqrt{2211}}{8}$

11. Subtract $\frac{23}{8}$ from both sides:

Answer: $\lambda =\frac{i \sqrt{2211}}{8}-\frac{23}{8}\text{ or }\lambda =-\frac{i \sqrt{2211}}{8}-\frac{23}{8}$

```

Insert an error into step 7.
