Given the following problem:

```

Find all the eigenvalues of the matrix $M:$

$M=\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
-8 & -5 & -5 \\
 6 & -2 & 7 \\
 9 & 7 & -9 \\
\end{array}
\right)$

```

And correct solution:

```

1. Find $\lambda \in \mathbb{C}\text{ such that }M v=\lambda  v\text{ for some nonzero vector }v:$

$M v=\lambda  v$

2. Rewrite the equation $M v=\lambda  v\text{ as }(M-\mathbb{I} \lambda ) v=0:$

$(M-\mathbb{I} \lambda ) v=0$

3. Find all $\lambda\text{  such that }| M-\mathbb{I} \lambda | =0:$

$| M-\mathbb{I} \lambda | =0$

4. $\begin{array}{lll}
M-\mathbb{I} \lambda  & = & \left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
-8 & -5 & -5 \\
 6 & -2 & 7 \\
 9 & 7 & -9 \\
\end{array}
\right)-\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
1 & 0 & 0 \\
 0 & 1 & 0 \\
 0 & 0 & 1 \\
\end{array}
\right) \lambda  \\
\text{  }& = & \left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
-\lambda -8 & -5 & -5 \\
 6 & -\lambda -2 & 7 \\
 9 & 7 & -\lambda -9 \\
\end{array}
\right) \\
\end{array}$

$\left| 
\begin{array}{ccc}
-\lambda -8 & -5 & -5 \\
 6 & -\lambda -2 & 7 \\
 9 & 7 & -\lambda -9 \\
\end{array}
\right| =0$

5. $\left| 
\begin{array}{ccc}
-\lambda -8 & -5 & -5 \\
 6 & -\lambda -2 & 7 \\
 9 & 7 & -\lambda -9 \\
\end{array}
\right| =-\lambda ^3-19 \lambda ^2-132 \lambda -637:$

$-\lambda ^3-19 \lambda ^2-132 \lambda -637=0$

6. Solving the equation $-\lambda ^3-19 \lambda ^2-132 \lambda -637=0\text{ for }\lambda$ yields the following eigenvalues:

Answer: $\lambda =-12.5208\text{ or }\lambda =-3.23959-6.35455 i\text{ or }\lambda =-3.23959+6.35455 i$

```

Insert an error into step 1.
