Given the following problem:

```
Simplify the following:

$\left(\sqrt{55}+\sqrt{75}\right) \sqrt{55}$
```

And correct solution:

```
1. $\sqrt{75} = \sqrt{3\times 5^2} = 5 \sqrt{3}$:

$\left(\sqrt{55}+5 \sqrt{3}\right) \sqrt{55}$

2. $\sqrt{55} \left(5 \sqrt{3}+\sqrt{55}\right)=\sqrt{55}\times 5 \sqrt{3}+\sqrt{55} \sqrt{55}$:

$\sqrt{55}\times 5 \sqrt{3}+\sqrt{55} \sqrt{55}$

3. $\sqrt{55} \sqrt{3}=\sqrt{55\times 3}$:

$\sqrt{55\times 3} 5+\sqrt{55} \sqrt{55}$

4. $55\times 3 = 165:$

$\sqrt{165}\times 5+\sqrt{55} \sqrt{55}$

5. $\sqrt{55} \sqrt{55}=55:$

$\sqrt{165}\times 5+55$

6. Factor $5\text{ out of }55+5 \sqrt{165}\text{ giving }5 \left(\sqrt{165}+11\right):$

Answer: $5 \left(\sqrt{165}+11\right)$
```

Insert an error into step 3.
