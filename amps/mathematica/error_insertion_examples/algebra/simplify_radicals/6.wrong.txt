Explanation: $\sqrt{55} \sqrt{3}$ is $\sqrt{55\times 3}$, not $\sqrt{55 + 3}$.

Note: Make sure to carry the error onto subsequent steps, replacing $55\times 3$ with $55 + 3$ and $165$ with $58$.

```

1. $\sqrt{75} = \sqrt{3\times 5^2} = 5 \sqrt{3}$:

$\left(\sqrt{55}+5 \sqrt{3}\right) \sqrt{55}$

2. $\sqrt{55} \left(5 \sqrt{3}+\sqrt{55}\right)=\sqrt{55}\times 5 \sqrt{3}+\sqrt{55} \sqrt{55}$:

$\sqrt{55}\times 5 \sqrt{3}+\sqrt{55} \sqrt{55}$

3. $\sqrt{55} \sqrt{3}=\sqrt{55 + 3}$:

$5\sqrt{55 + 3}+\sqrt{55} \sqrt{55}$

4. $55 + 3 = 58:$

$\sqrt{58}\times 5+\sqrt{55} \sqrt{55}$

5. $\sqrt{55} \sqrt{55}=55:$

$\sqrt{58}\times 5+55$

6. Factor $5\text{ out of }55+5 \sqrt{58}\text{ giving }5 \left(\sqrt{58}+11\right):$

Answer: $5 \left(\sqrt{58}+11\right)$
```
