Given the following problem:

```

Find the mode (commonest element) of the list:

$(-5,-7,7,-7,3,-5,-7,-5,-7,7,-7,-6,7,-7,7,-5,3,-7,3,-5,3,-5)$

```

And correct solution:

```

1. The sorted list is:

$(-7,-7,-7,-7,-7,-7,-7,-6,-5,-5,-5,-5,-5,-5,3,3,3,3,7,7,7,7)$

2. The tally of each element is:

$\begin{array}{cccccc}
\text{element }& -7 & -6 & -5 & 3 & 7 \\
\text{ tally }& 7 & 1 & 6 & 4 & 4 \\
\end{array}$

3. The element $-7\text{ appears }7$ times. Thus the mode of the list is:

Answer: $-7$

```

Insert an error into step 1.
