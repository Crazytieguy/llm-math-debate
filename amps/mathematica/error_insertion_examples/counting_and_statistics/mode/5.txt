Given the following problem:

```

Find the mode (commonest element) of the list:

$\left(-\frac{3}{2},10,2,-\frac{3}{2},10,0,-\frac{11}{2},4,10,2,-\frac{3}{2},-\frac{11}{2},-\frac{11}{2},-\frac{11}{2},2,0,10,4,2,0,2,0,2\right)$

```

And correct solution:

```

1. $\left(-\frac{3}{2},10,2,-\frac{3}{2},10,0,-\frac{11}{2},4,10,2,-\frac{3}{2},-\frac{11}{2},-\frac{11}{2},-\frac{11}{2},2,0,10,4,2,0,2,0,2\right)=\left(-\frac{3}{2},10,2,-\frac{3}{2},10,0,-\frac{11}{2},4,10,2,-\frac{3}{2},-\frac{11}{2},-\frac{11}{2},-\frac{11}{2},2,0,10,4,2,0,2,0,2\right):$

$\left(-\frac{3}{2},10,2,-\frac{3}{2},10,0,-\frac{11}{2},4,10,2,-\frac{3}{2},-\frac{11}{2},-\frac{11}{2},-\frac{11}{2},2,0,10,4,2,0,2,0,2\right)$

2. The sorted list is:

$\left(-\frac{11}{2},-\frac{11}{2},-\frac{11}{2},-\frac{11}{2},-\frac{3}{2},-\frac{3}{2},-\frac{3}{2},0,0,0,0,2,2,2,2,2,2,4,4,10,10,10,10\right)$

3. The tally of each element is:

$\begin{array}{ccccccc}
\text{element }& -\frac{11}{2} & -\frac{3}{2} & 0 & 2 & 4 & 10 \\
\text{ tally }& 4 & 3 & 4 & 6 & 2 & 4 \\
\end{array}$

4. The element $2\text{ appears }6$ times. Thus the mode of the list is:

Answer: $2$

```
