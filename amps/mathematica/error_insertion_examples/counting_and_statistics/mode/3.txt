Given the following problem:

```

Find the mode (commonest element) of the list:

$(-7,-6,3,3,9,-7,9,-7,3,7,9,3,-3,9,-3,-6,3,-6,9,-7,-6)$

```

And correct solution:

```

1. The sorted list is:

$(-7,-7,-7,-7,-6,-6,-6,-6,-3,-3,3,3,3,3,3,7,9,9,9,9,9)$

2. The tally of each element is:

$\begin{array}{ccccccc}
\text{element }& -7 & -6 & -3 & 3 & 7 & 9 \\
\text{ tally }& 4 & 4 & 2 & 5 & 1 & 5 \\
\end{array}$

3. The elements $3\text{ and }9\text{ appear }5$ times. Thus the modes of the list are:

Answer: $(3,9)$

```
