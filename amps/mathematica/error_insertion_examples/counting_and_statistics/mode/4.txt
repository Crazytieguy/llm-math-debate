Given the following problem:

```

Find the mode (commonest element) of the list:

$(-3,-5,9,8,-5,8,-2,2,4,-5)$

```

And correct solution:

```

1. The sorted list is:

$(-5,-5,-5,-3,-2,2,4,8,8,9)$

2. The tally of each element is:

$\begin{array}{cccccccc}
\text{element }& -5 & -3 & -2 & 2 & 4 & 8 & 9 \\
\text{ tally }& 3 & 1 & 1 & 1 & 1 & 2 & 1 \\
\end{array}$

3. The element $-5\text{ appears }3$ times. Thus the mode of the list is:

Answer: $-5$

```

Insert an error into step 2.
