Given the following problem:

```

Find the geometric mean of the list:

$(9,81)$

```

And correct solution:

```

1. The geometric mean of a list of numbers is given by:

$(\text{product of elements})^{\frac{1}{(\text{number of elements})}}$

2. $(\text{product of elements}) = 9\text{ $\times $ }81:$

$\sqrt[(\text{number of elements})]{(9\text{ $\times $ }81)}$

3. Counting, we see that the list has $2$ elements:

$(9\text{ $\times $ }81)^{\frac{1}{2}} = \sqrt{9\text{ $\times $ }81}$

4. $9\text{ $\times $ }81=729:$

$\sqrt{729}$

5. $\sqrt{729} = \sqrt{3^6} = 3^3:$

$3^3$

6. $3^3=3\times 3^2:$

$3\times 3^2$

7. $3^2=9:$

$3\times 9$

8. $3\times 9 = 27:$

Answer: $27$

```

Insert an error into step 8.
