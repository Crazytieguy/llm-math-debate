Given the following problem:

```

Find the mean of the list:

$(-9,-4,7,-2,-9,7)$

```

And correct solution:

```

1. The mean of a list of numbers is given by:

$\frac{(\text{sum of elements})}{(\text{number of elements})}$

2. $(\text{sum of elements}) = -9-4+7-2-9+7:$

$\frac{-9-4+7-2-9+7}{(\text{number of elements})}$

3. Counting, we see that the list has $6$ elements:

$\frac{-9-4+7-2-9+7}{6}$

4. $-9-4+7-2-9+7 = -10:$

$\frac{-10}{6}$

5. $\frac{-10}{6}=-\frac{5}{3}$:

Answer: $-\frac{5}{3}\approx -1.66667$

```

Insert an error into step 3.
