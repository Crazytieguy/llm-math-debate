Given the following problem:

```

Compute the Euler phi function for $4676:$

$\phi (4676)$

```

And correct solution:

```

1. Apply the the Euler phi function definition to $4676:$

$\phi (4676)\text{ is the number of elements of }\{1,2,\ldots ,4676\}\text{ that are coprime to }4676$

2. For a positive integer $n, \phi (n)=n \left(\prod _{\text{p $|$ n}} \left(1-\frac{1}{p}\right)\right),\text{ where the product is over the distinct prime numbers that divide }n:$

$\phi (4676)=4676 \prod _{\text{p $|$ 4676}} \left(1-\frac{1}{p}\right)$

3. The prime factorization of 4676 is $2^2\times 7\times 167,$ so the prime factors of 4676 are:

$2,7\text{ and }167$

4. Substituting the prime factors into the Euler product formula gives:

Answer: $\phi (4676)=\left(4676 \left(1-\frac{1}{2}\right) \left(1-\frac{1}{7}\right) \left(1-\frac{1}{167}\right)=1992\right)$

```
