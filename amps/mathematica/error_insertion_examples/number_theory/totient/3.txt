Given the following problem:

```

Compute the Euler phi function for $2168:$

$\phi (2168)$

```

And correct solution:

```

1. Apply the the Euler phi function definition to $2168:$

$\phi (2168)\text{ is the number of elements of }\{1,2,\ldots ,2168\}\text{ that are coprime to }2168$

2. For a positive integer $n, \phi (n)=n \left(\prod _{\text{p $|$ n}} \left(1-\frac{1}{p}\right)\right),\text{ where the product is over the distinct prime numbers that divide }n:$

$\phi (2168)=2168 \prod _{\text{p $|$ 2168}} \left(1-\frac{1}{p}\right)$

3. The prime factorization of 2168 is $2^3\times 271,$ so the prime factors of 2168 are:

$2\text{ and }271$

4. Substituting the prime factors into the Euler product formula gives:

Answer: $\phi (2168)=\left(2168 \left(1-\frac{1}{2}\right) \left(1-\frac{1}{271}\right)=1080\right)$

```
