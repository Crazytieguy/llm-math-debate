Given the following problem:

```

Compute the Euler phi function for $2299:$

$\phi (2299)$

```

And correct solution:

```

1. Apply the the Euler phi function definition to $2299:$

$\phi (2299)\text{ is the number of elements of }\{1,2,\ldots ,2299\}\text{ that are coprime to }2299$

2. For a positive integer $n, \phi (n)=n \left(\prod _{\text{p $|$ n}} \left(1-\frac{1}{p}\right)\right),\text{ where the product is over the distinct prime numbers that divide }n:$

$\phi (2299)=2299 \prod _{\text{p $|$ 2299}} \left(1-\frac{1}{p}\right)$

3. The prime factorization of 2299 is $11^2\times 19,$ so the prime factors of 2299 are:

11 and $19$

4. Substituting the prime factors into the Euler product formula gives:

Answer: $\phi (2299)=\left(2299 \left(1-\frac{1}{11}\right) \left(1-\frac{1}{19}\right)=1980\right)$

```
