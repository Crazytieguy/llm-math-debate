$x^2+\frac{13 x}{11}=\frac{5}{11}$

$x^2+\frac{13 x}{11}+\frac{169}{484}=\frac{389}{484}$

$x+\frac{13}{22}=\frac{\sqrt{389}}{22}\text{ or }x+\frac{13}{22}=-\frac{\sqrt{389}}{22}$
