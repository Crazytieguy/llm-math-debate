Solve the following system:

$\{
\begin{array}{l}
-\frac{146 y}{7}-\frac{46 x}{7}-\frac{34}{7}=0 \\
 -4 y+2 x-\frac{108}{7}=0 \\
\end{array}$

\hrule

1. In the first equation, look to solve for $x:$

$\{
\begin{array}{l}
-\frac{146 y}{7}-\frac{46 x}{7}-\frac{34}{7}=0 \\
 -4 y+2 x-\frac{108}{7}=0 \\
\end{array}$

2. Bring $-\frac{146 y}{7}-\frac{46 x}{7}-\frac{34}{7}\text{ together using the common denominator }7:$

$\{
\begin{array}{l}
-\frac{2}{7} (73 y+23 x+17)=0 \\
 -4 y+2 x-\frac{108}{7}=0 \\
\end{array}$

3. Multiply both sides by $-\frac{7}{2}$:

$\{
\begin{array}{l}
73 y+23 x+17=0 \\
 -4 y+2 x-\frac{108}{7}=0 \\
\end{array}$

4. Subtract $73 y+17$ from both sides:

$\{
\begin{array}{l}
23 x=-73 y-17 \\
 -4 y+2 x-\frac{108}{7}=0 \\
\end{array}$

5. Divide both sides by $23:$

$\{
\begin{array}{l}
x=-\frac{73 y}{23}-\frac{17}{23} \\
 -4 y+2 x-\frac{108}{7}=0 \\
\end{array}$

6. Substitute $x=-\frac{73 y}{23}-\frac{17}{23}$ into the second equation:

$\{
\begin{array}{l}
x=-\frac{73 y}{23}-\frac{17}{23} \\
 -\frac{108}{7}+2 \left(-\frac{73 y}{23}-\frac{17}{23}\right)-4 y=0 \\
\end{array}$

7. $-\frac{108}{7}+2 \left(\frac{-73 y}{23}-\frac{17}{23}\right)-4 y=-4 y+\left(-\frac{146 y}{23}-\frac{34}{23}\right)-\frac{108}{7}=-\frac{238 y}{23}-\frac{2722}{161}$:

$\{
\begin{array}{l}
x=-\frac{73 y}{23}-\frac{17}{23} \\
 -\frac{238 y}{23}-\frac{2722}{161}=0 \\
\end{array}$

8. In the second equation, look to solve for $y:$

$\{
\begin{array}{l}
x=-\frac{73 y}{23}-\frac{17}{23} \\
 -\frac{238 y}{23}-\frac{2722}{161}=0 \\
\end{array}$

9. Bring $-\frac{238 y}{23}-\frac{2722}{161}\text{ together using the common denominator }161:$

$\{
\begin{array}{l}
x=-\frac{73 y}{23}-\frac{17}{23} \\
 -\frac{2}{161} (833 y+1361)=0 \\
\end{array}$

10. Multiply both sides by $-\frac{161}{2}$:

$\{
\begin{array}{l}
x=-\frac{73 y}{23}-\frac{17}{23} \\
 833 y+1361=0 \\
\end{array}$

11. Subtract 1361 from both sides:

$\{
\begin{array}{l}
x=-\frac{73 y}{23}-\frac{17}{23} \\
 833 y=-1361 \\
\end{array}$

12. Divide both sides by $833:$

$\{
\begin{array}{l}
x=-\frac{73 y}{23}-\frac{17}{23} \\
 y=-\frac{1361}{833} \\
\end{array}$

13. Substitute $y=-\frac{1361}{833}$ into the first equation:
