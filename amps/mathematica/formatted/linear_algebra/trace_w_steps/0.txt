Find the trace of the matrix:

$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
-3 e & -4 e & -2 e & -2 e \\
 -2 e & -2 e & -4 e & -4 e \\
 e & 3 e & 2 e & -3 e \\
 -2 e & 3 e & -3 e & 3 e \\
\end{array}
\right)$

\hrule

1. Locate the elements on the main diagonal:

$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
-3 e & -4 e & -2 e & -2 e \\
 -2 e & -2 e & -4 e & -4 e \\
 e & 3 e & 2 e & -3 e \\
 -2 e & 3 e & -3 e & 3 e \\
\end{array}
\right)$

2. Write out the sum of the elements on the main diagonal:

$-3 e\,\text{ +$\, $}-2 e\,\text{ +$\, $}2 e\,\text{ +$\, $}3 e$

3. Add like terms. $-3 e-2 e+2 e+3 e = 0:$

Answer: $0$
