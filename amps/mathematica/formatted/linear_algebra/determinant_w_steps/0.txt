Find the determinant:

$\left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
-2 & -5 \\
 2 & -4 \\
\end{array}
\right|$

\hrule

1. Multiply along the diagonals and subtract:

$(-2)\, (-4)-(-5)\, \times \, 2$

2. $-2 (-4) = 8:$

$8--5\times 2$

3. $-(-5) = 5:$

$8+5\times 2$

4. $5\times 2 = 10:$

$8+10$

5. $8+10=18:$

Answer: $18$
