Find the range of the list:

$\left(-5,\frac{3}{2},-4,-\frac{279}{32},-\frac{185}{64},-4,-\frac{271}{32},-\frac{113}{16},7,5,-8,9,-4,-3,1\right) = \left(-5,\frac{3}{2},-4,-\frac{279}{32},-\frac{185}{64},-4,-\frac{271}{32},-\frac{113}{16},7,5,-8,9,-4,-3,1\right)$

\hrule

1. The range of a list is given by:

$(\text{largest element})-(\text{smallest element})$

2. $\left(-5,\frac{3}{2},-4,-\frac{279}{32},-\frac{185}{64},-4,-\frac{271}{32},-\frac{113}{16},7,5,-8,9,-4,-3,1\right)\text{  We see that the largest element is }9:$

$9-(\text{smallest element})$

3. $\left(-5,\frac{3}{2},-4,-\frac{279}{32},-\frac{185}{64},-4,-\frac{271}{32},-\frac{113}{16},7,5,-8,9,-4,-3,1\right)\text{  We see that the smallest element is }-\frac{279}{32}$:

$-\frac{-279}{32}+9$

4. $-\frac{-279}{32}+9=\frac{567}{32}$:

Answer: $\frac{567}{32}$
