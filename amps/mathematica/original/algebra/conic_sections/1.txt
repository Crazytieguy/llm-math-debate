Problem:
Given the equation $-x^2+3 y^2-5 y+4=0$, rewrite and classify the conic and state relevant properties.
Answer:
Classification: Hyperbola
Equation: $3 \left(y-\frac{5}{6}\right)^2-x^2=-\frac{23}{12}$
Foci: $\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
 -\frac{\sqrt{23}}{3} & \frac{5}{6} \\
 \frac{\sqrt{23}}{3} & \frac{5}{6} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Eccentricity: $\frac{2}{\sqrt{3}}$
Center: $\left\{0,\frac{5}{6}\right\}$
Asymptotes: $\left\{y=\frac{x}{\sqrt{3}}+\frac{5}{6},y=\frac{5}{6}-\frac{x}{\sqrt{3}}\right\}$
