Problem:
Compute the sample variance of ${8, -6, -13}$.
Answer:
\begin{array}{l}
 
\begin{array}{l}
 \text{Find the (sample) variance of the list}: \\
 (8,-6,-13)\text{} \\
\end{array}
 \\
\hline
 
\begin{array}{l}
 \text{The }\text{(sample) }\text{variance }\text{of }\text{a }\text{list }\text{of }\text{numbers }X=\left\{X_1,X_2,\ldots ,X_n\right\} \text{with }\text{mean }\mu =\frac{X_1+X_2+\ldots +X_n}{n} \text{is }\text{given }\text{by}: \\
 \frac{\left| X_1-\mu \right| {}^2+\left| X_2-\mu \right| {}^2+\ldots +\left| X_n-\mu \right| {}^2}{n-1} \\
\end{array}
 \\
 
\begin{array}{l}
 \text{There }\text{are }n=3 \text{elements }\text{in }\text{the }\text{list }X=\{8,-6,-13\}: \\
 \frac{\left| X_1-\mu \right| {}^2+\left| X_2-\mu \right| {}^2+\left| X_3-\mu \right| {}^2}{3-1} \\
\end{array}
 \\
 
\begin{array}{l}
 
\begin{array}{l}
 \text{The }\text{elements }X_i \text{of }\text{the }\text{list }X=\{8,-6,-13\} \text{are:} \\
 
\begin{array}{l}
 X_1=8 \\
 X_2=-6 \\
 X_3=-13 \\
\end{array}
 \\
\end{array}
 \\
 \frac{| 8-\mu | ^2+| -\mu -6| ^2+| -\mu -13| ^2}{3-1} \\
\end{array}
 \\
 
\begin{array}{l}
 \text{The }\text{mean }(\mu ) \text{is }\text{given }\text{by$\backslash $n$\backslash $n}\mu =\frac{X_1+X_2+X_3}{3}=\frac{8-6-13}{3}=-\frac{11}{3}: \\
 \frac{\left| 8-\left(-\frac{11}{3}\right)\right| ^2+\left| -\frac{-11}{3}-6\right| ^2+\left| -\frac{-11}{3}-13\right| ^2}{3-1} \\
\end{array}
 \\
 
\begin{array}{l}
 
\begin{array}{l}
 \text{The values of the differences are:} \\
 
\begin{array}{l}
 -\frac{-11}{3}+8=\frac{35}{3} \\
 -\frac{-11}{3}-6=-\frac{7}{3} \\
 -\frac{-11}{3}-13=-\frac{28}{3} \\
 3-1=2 \\
\end{array}
 \\
\end{array}
 \\
 \frac{\left| \frac{35}{3}\right| ^2+\left| -\frac{7}{3}\right| ^2+\left| -\frac{28}{3}\right| ^2}{2} \\
\end{array}
 \\
 
\begin{array}{l}
 
\begin{array}{l}
 \text{The }\text{values }\text{of }\text{the }\text{terms }\text{in }\text{the }\text{numerator }\text{are}: \\
 
\begin{array}{l}
 \left| \frac{35}{3}\right| ^2 \text{= }\frac{1225}{9} \\
 \left| -\frac{7}{3}\right| ^2 \text{= }\frac{49}{9} \\
 \left| -\frac{28}{3}\right| ^2 \text{= }\frac{784}{9} \\
\end{array}
 \\
\end{array}
 \\
 \frac{\frac{1225}{9}+\frac{49}{9}+\frac{784}{9}}{2} \\
\end{array}
 \\
 
\begin{array}{l}
 \frac{1225}{9}+\frac{49}{9}+\frac{784}{9}=\frac{686}{3}: \\
 \frac{\frac{686}{3}}{2} \\
\end{array}
 \\
 
\begin{array}{l}
 \frac{\frac{686}{3}}{2}=\frac{343}{3}: \\
 \fbox{$
\begin{array}{ll}
 \text{Answer:} &  \\
 \text{} & \frac{343}{3} \\
\end{array}
$} \\
\end{array}
 \\
\end{array}
