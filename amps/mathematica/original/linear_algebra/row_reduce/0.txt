Problem:
Convert the following matrix to reduced row echelon form:
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 3 & 5 & 2 & 10 \\
 -5 & -2 & 9 & 3 \\
 0 & 1 & -6 & -6 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 1 & 0 & 0 & \frac{168}{151} \\
 0 & 1 & 0 & \frac{132}{151} \\
 0 & 0 & 1 & \frac{173}{151} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
