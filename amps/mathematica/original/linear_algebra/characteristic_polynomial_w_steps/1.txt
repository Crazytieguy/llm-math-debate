Problem:
Find the characteristic polynomial of the following matrix:
$\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
 -\frac{29}{4} & -\frac{27}{4} \\
 -\frac{5}{2} & -1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
\begin{array}{l}
 
\begin{array}{l}
 \text{Find the characteristic polynomial of the matrix }M\text{ with respect to the variable }\lambda : \\
 M=\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
 -\frac{29}{4} & -\frac{27}{4} \\
 -\frac{5}{2} & -1 \\
\end{array}
\right) \\
\end{array}
 \\
\hline
 
\begin{array}{l}
 \text{To }\text{find }\text{the }\text{characteristic }\text{polynomial }\text{of }\text{a }\text{matrix, }\text{subtract }\text{a }\text{variable }\text{multiplied }\text{by }\text{the }\text{identity }\text{matrix }\text{and }\text{take }\text{the }\text{determinant}: \\
 | M-\lambda  \mathbb{I}|  \\
\end{array}
 \\
 
\begin{array}{l}
 
\begin{array}{lll}
 | M-\lambda  \mathbb{I}|  & = & \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
 -\frac{29}{4} & -\frac{27}{4} \\
 -\frac{5}{2} & -1 \\
\end{array}
-\lambda  
\begin{array}{cc}
 1 & 0 \\
 0 & 1 \\
\end{array}
\right|  \\
 \text{} & = & \left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
 -\frac{29}{4} & -\frac{27}{4} \\
 -\frac{5}{2} & -1 \\
\end{array}
-
\begin{array}{cc}
 \lambda  & 0 \\
 0 & \lambda  \\
\end{array}
\right|  \\
\end{array}
 \\
  \text{= }\left| 
\begin{array}{cc}
 -\lambda -\frac{29}{4} & -\frac{27}{4} \\
 -\frac{5}{2} & -\lambda -1 \\
\end{array}
\right|  \\
\end{array}
 \\
 
\begin{array}{l}
 \text{Multiply }\text{along }\text{the }\text{diagonals }\text{and }\text{subtract}: \\
 \left(-\lambda -\frac{29}{4}\right) (-\lambda -1)-\left(-\frac{27}{4}\right)\, \left(-\frac{5}{2}\right) \\
\end{array}
 \\
 
\begin{array}{l}
 \left(-\lambda -\frac{29}{4}\right) (-\lambda -1)=\lambda ^2+\frac{33 \lambda }{4}+\frac{29}{4}: \\
  \text{= }\frac{29}{4}+\frac{33 \lambda }{4}+\lambda ^2-\left(-\frac{27}{4}\right)\, \left(-\frac{5}{2}\right) \\
\end{array}
 \\
 
\begin{array}{l}
 \frac{-27}{4}\times \frac{-5}{2}=\frac{135}{8}: \\
  \text{= }\left(\lambda ^2+\frac{33 \lambda }{4}+\frac{29}{4}\right)-\frac{135}{8} \\
\end{array}
 \\
 
\begin{array}{l}
 \left(\lambda ^2+\frac{33 \lambda }{4}+\frac{29}{4}\right)-\frac{135}{8}=\lambda ^2+\frac{33 \lambda }{4}-\frac{77}{8}: \\
 \fbox{$
\begin{array}{ll}
 \text{Answer:} &  \\
 \text{} &  \text{= }\lambda ^2+\frac{33 \lambda }{4}-\frac{77}{8} \\
\end{array}
$} \\
\end{array}
 \\
\end{array}
