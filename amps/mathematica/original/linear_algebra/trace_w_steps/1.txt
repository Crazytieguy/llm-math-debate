Problem:
Compute the trace of
$\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
 9 & -2 \\
 -4 & 7 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
\begin{array}{l}
 
\begin{array}{l}
 \text{Find the trace of the matrix:} \\
 \left(
\begin{array}{cc}
 9 & -2 \\
 -4 & 7 \\
\end{array}
\right) \\
\end{array}
 \\
\hline
 
\begin{array}{l}
 \text{Locate }\text{the }\text{elements }\text{on }\text{the }\text{main }\text{diagonal}: \\
 \left(
\begin{array}{cc}
 9 & -2 \\
 -4 & 7 \\
\end{array}
\right) \\
\end{array}
 \\
 
\begin{array}{l}
 \text{Write }\text{out }\text{the }\text{sum }\text{of }\text{the }\text{elements }\text{on }\text{the }\text{main }\text{diagonal}: \\
 9\, \text{+$\, $}7 \\
\end{array}
 \\
 
\begin{array}{l}
 9+7=16: \\
 \fbox{$
\begin{array}{ll}
 \text{Answer:} &  \\
 \text{} & 16 \\
\end{array}
$} \\
\end{array}
 \\
\end{array}
