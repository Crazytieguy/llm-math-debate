Problem:
Multiply the scalar $\frac{9}{100}$ and the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 9 & 1 & 6 & -10 \\
 -2 & 8 & -3 & -10 \\
 -7 & 9 & -3 & 5 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 \frac{81}{100} & \frac{9}{100} & \frac{27}{50} & -\frac{9}{10} \\
 -\frac{9}{50} & \frac{18}{25} & -\frac{27}{100} & -\frac{9}{10} \\
 -\frac{63}{100} & \frac{81}{100} & -\frac{27}{100} & \frac{9}{20} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
