Problem:
Multiply the scalar $\frac{9}{8}$ and the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 -7 & 1 & 7 & 4 \\
 1 & 1 & 9 & 8 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 -\frac{63}{8} & \frac{9}{8} & \frac{63}{8} & \frac{9}{2} \\
 \frac{9}{8} & \frac{9}{8} & \frac{81}{8} & 9 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
