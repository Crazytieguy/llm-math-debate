Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 0 & 1 & -2 \\
 -1 & 0 & -1 \\
 1 & 2 & -2 \\
 2 & -1 & -1 \\
 3 & 3 & -1 \\
 3 & -3 & 3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.65 \\
 -0.19 \\
 -0.25 \\
 2.96 \\
 -0.09 \\
 2.24 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.599 \\
 -0.898 \\
 -0.691 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
