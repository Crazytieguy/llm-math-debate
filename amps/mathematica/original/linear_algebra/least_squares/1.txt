Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccccc}
 2 & 3 & 3 & 1 & 1 \\
 3 & 1 & 3 & -1 & 0 \\
 3 & -1 & 2 & 0 & -3 \\
 -2 & 2 & -3 & -1 & -2 \\
 3 & 1 & -2 & -3 & 2 \\
 0 & 2 & 2 & 2 & -2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 1.71 \\
 -1.87 \\
 0.14 \\
 -0.43 \\
 -2.46 \\
 -2.33 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.12 \\
 -0.24 \\
 0.008 \\
 0.482 \\
 0.257 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
