Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{10}{3} \\
 \frac{11}{3} \\
 -4 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{29}{3} \\
 -\frac{19}{3} \\
 -\frac{20}{3} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{448}{9} \\
 -\frac{548}{9} \\
 -\frac{43}{3} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
